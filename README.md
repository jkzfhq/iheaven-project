# The iHeaven Project (天庭计划)

> An Engineering Blueprint for the Perpetuity of Civilization

## What happens to our data when we're gone?

Our digital footprint is growing exponentially, but it's fragile. Stored on centralized servers, governed by terms of service we don't read, and ultimately, destined to be deleted. How do we, as builders, solve the problem of permanent, sovereign, and incorruptible data preservation for humanity?

## Our Answer: The iHeaven Project

This is an open-source initiative to build the **Library of Beings**: a decentralized network where every individual and culture can permanently preserve their story. We are building a system designed to last for centuries, governed by its users, and owned by no one, in order to combat the most relentless law of the universe—entropy.

---

### Core Philosophy: The Physics of Fighting Oblivion

The foundation of the iHeaven Project is not built on fantasy, but rooted in profound scientific and philosophical principles:

1.  **Fighting Entropy**: The universe's destiny is heat death and chaos. Life itself is a miracle that defies entropy, and the iHeaven Project elevates this struggle from a biological level to a cosmological one, building a "negative entropy ark" for our collective memory.
2.  **Information Immortality**: Physics ultimately tells us that information is conserved in the universe and cannot be utterly destroyed. The iHeaven Library draws inspiration from the stability of DNA and Shannon's information theory to design a new, more robust "digital DNA" for human civilization.
3.  **The Law of Decentralization**: From the mycelial networks beneath the forest floor to the cosmic web above, nature repeatedly reveals that decentralized systems are far more resilient and enduring than centralized ones. The iHeaven Library must be built on the bedrock of blockchain and distributed storage—this is the only path to permanence.

### Technical Architecture (How We're Building It)

-   **Storage Layer**: Leveraging permanent storage protocols like Arweave and Filecoin to ensure the physical distribution and durability of data.
-   **Consensus & Economic Layer**: Powered by the `love` token (SPL Token) on the Solana blockchain, providing economic incentives and value circulation for the entire ecosystem.
-   **Governance Layer**: A "separation of powers" DAO structure composed of the Community, the Foundation, and the Institute, adhering to a unique 33-year "Nirvana" cycle to prevent organizational stagnation.
-   **Consciousness Layer**: Exploring "Digital Human Agents" based on Large Language Models and Integrated Information Theory, allowing the data stored in the library to "come alive" as a new form of digital life.

### Roadmap: The First Epoch's Expedition (2025-2055)

This is a thirty-year construction plan, divided into four distinct phases:

-   **Phase I: Foundation (Years 1-3)**
    -   **Mission**: Complete a $3 million fundraising round, build the iHeaven online network, and establish the first three offline Heavenly Court Libraries.
-   **Phase II: Broadcast (Years 4-5)**
    -   **Mission**: Launch the first iHeaven satellite to back up data in space and pioneer a space broadcasting business model.
-   **Phase III: Service (Years 6-15)**
    -   **Mission**: Expand to 1,000 Heavenly Court Libraries worldwide, establishing a stable service network and cash flow, while continuously acquiring physical gold as a strategic reserve.
-   **Phase IV: Voyage (Years 16-30)**
    -   **Mission**: Devote all resources to the research, construction, and launch of the first unmanned starship, "iHeaven Ark One," carrying the "seeds of civilization."

# 天庭计划

> 一部关于文明永续的工程蓝图

## 当我们离去，数据将何去何从？

我们的数字足迹正在呈指数级增长，但它却无比脆弱。它储存在中心化的服务器上，受我们从未阅读的服务条款约束，最终的命运是被删除。我们作为建造者，该如何为人类解决永久、自主且不可篡改的数据保存问题？

## 我们的答案：天庭计划

这是一个旨在构建**众生图书馆**的开源计划：一个去中心化的网络，让每个个体与文化都能永久保存自己的故事。我们正在构建一个旨在延续数个世纪、由用户治理、不被任何人拥有的系统，以此对抗宇宙最无情的法则——熵增。

---

### 核心理念：对抗遗忘的物理学

天庭计划的基石并非凭空想象，而是植根于深刻的科学与哲学原理：

1.  **对抗熵增**：宇宙的宿命是走向热寂与混乱。生命本身是一场逆熵而行的奇迹，而天庭计划，是将这场战斗从生物学提升到文明宇宙学的层面，为我们的集体记忆建造一艘“负熵方舟”。
2.  **信息永生**：物理学最终告诉我们，信息在宇宙中是守恒的，无法被彻底摧毁。天庭图书馆借鉴了 DNA 的稳定与夏农的信息论，旨在为人类文明设计一种全新的、更稳健的“数字 DNA”。
3.  **去中心化法则**：从地下的菌丝体网络到天上的宇宙网，大自然反复揭示：去中心化的系统远比中心化的更坚韧、更持久。天庭图书馆必须建立在区块链和分布式储存的基石之上，这是通往永恒的唯一路径。

### 技术架构

-   **存储层**: 利用 Arweave、Filecoin 等永久性存储协议，确保数据的物理分散与持久性。
-   **共识与经济层**: 由基于 Solana 区塊鏈的 `love` Token 驱动，为整个生态提供经济激励与价值流转。
-   **治理层**: 一个由社区、基金会、研究院构成的“三权分立”DAO 结构，并遵循独特的三十三年“涅槃”法则，确保组织永不僵化。
-   **意识层**: 探索基于大型语言模型与信息整合理论的“数字人智能体”，让储存在图书馆中的数据“活”起来，成为一种全新的数字生命形式。

### 路线图：第一纪的远征 (2025-2055)

这是一个跨越三十年的建造计划，分为四个清晰的阶段：

-   **第一阶段：奠基 (第 1-3 年)**
    -   **任务**: 完成 300 万美元募资，建成 iHeaven 线上网络与首批 3 个线下天庭图书馆。
-   **第二阶段：广播 (第 4-5 年)**
    -   **任务**: 发射第一颗天庭人造卫星，将数据备份至太空，并开创太空广播商业模式。
-   **第三阶段：服务 (第 6-15 年)**
    -   **任务**: 在全球扩展至 1000 家天庭图书馆，建立稳定的服务网络与现金流，并持续购入黄金作为战略储备。
-   **第四阶段：远航 (第 16-30 年)**
    -   **任务**: 倾尽全力研发、建造并发射第一艘承载“文明种子”的无人宇宙飞船「天庭方舟一号」。
